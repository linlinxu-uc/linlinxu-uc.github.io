Government Agencies

Canada

  • Natural Resources Canada - Canada's national resource department with extensive geospatial data and tools
  • Canadian Ice Service - Monitoring ice conditions across Canadian waters
  • Canadian Geodetic Survey - Canada's official geodetic reference systems
  • Statistics Canada - National statistical agency with geospatial data products
  • Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada - National Agri-Environmental Health Analysis and Reporting Program
  • Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council of Canada (NSERC) - Funding agency for geospatial research

International

  • Canadian Space Agency (CSA) - Canada's space program with Earth observation missions
  • European Space Agency (ESA) - Europe's space program with Earth observation missions
  • ESA Earth Online - Earth observation data portal
  • NOAA - National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration
  • Copernicus Programme - European Earth observation program
  • UN-SPIDER - United Nations Platform for Space-based Information for Disaster Management and Emergency Response

Industry & Commercial

  • ESRI - Leading GIS software company (ArcGIS)
  • Maxar Technologies - Satellite imagery and geospatial solutions
  • Planet Labs - Daily global satellite imagery
  • Hexagon Geospatial - Geospatial software solutions
  • Descartes Labs - Geospatial AI platform
